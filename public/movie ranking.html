The Best Movie According To Sandeepan

My Favourite Top 3 Movies Of All Time

The Road

The movie takes place in a post apocalyptic world. The story is a very emotional one , it shows the survival story of a father and a son

The Green Mile

This is a true story which dives into racial discrimination faced by african people in u.s.a and the story of how a friendship is formed between two unusual people

Watchmen

One of the best superhero movie based on the graphic novel of the same name written by alan moore. It was way ahead of its time
